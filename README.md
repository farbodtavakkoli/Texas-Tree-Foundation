## Project Purpose

Texas Trees Foundation has worked for decades to improve urban environments through tree planting, canopy expansion, and science-based planning. This repository supports that mission by providing data-driven insights into:

- Urban heat and microclimate conditions  
- Air quality patterns and temporal dynamics  
- The environmental benefits of trees and shade  
- Community-scale environmental exposure  

The analyses are intended to inform program design, evaluation, and policy-relevant decision-making.
